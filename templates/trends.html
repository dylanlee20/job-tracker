How It Works
  1. System captures job market snapshot every Sunday at 2:00 AM
  2. Tracks totals by category, company, and location
  3. Calculates week-over-week changes (new vs closed positions)
  4. Note: First 4 weeks reflect baseline building, not market changes
  5. After 52 weeks, enables year-over-year comparisons
  6. Historical data reveals hiring trends invisible to others
